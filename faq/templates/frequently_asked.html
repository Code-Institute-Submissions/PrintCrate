Frequently Asked Questions

Can I make a bulk order?

Standard stock levels are based on previous customer demand, if you require a greater quantity than is available please contact us.

Can I purchase design files (STL files) of your products?

We don't currently offer purchasing of standard design files, we can however provide design files of your own custom orders.

What plastics are used in your products?

PETG, a Glycol modified version of Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET) is primarily used. PETG is suitable for printing across a wide variety of applications as it is durable, strong and impact resistance while maintaining lower printing costs.

Are your products food-safe?

We cannot provide food-safe/medical grade products due to the nature of FDM printing, custom orders of products for these environments will be advised against their use past educational purposes.
